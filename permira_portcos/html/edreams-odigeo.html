eDreams ODIGEO
With five leading brands – Opodo, eDreams, GO Voyages, Travelink and Liligo – eDreams ODIEGO has pioneered online travel booking, including Prime, the largest subscription program in the travel segment.

Europe’s largest flight retailer, the company has a presence in over 45 global travel markets, providing consumers with the tools to find their product of choice across destinations.
