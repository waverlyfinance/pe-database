“We have long admired the AltamarCAM team for their deep client relationships, continued innovation as a leading European solution provider and their ability to grow in key strategic segments. We look forward to this partnership and the team’s success ahead.”

The company helps Institutional Investors, Family Offices and Private Banks to navigate the complexities of investing in Private Assets, by providing access to multi-asset classes including Private Equity, Secondaries, Venture Capital, Life Sciences, Real Estate, Infrastructure or Credit
