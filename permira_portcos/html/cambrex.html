Cambrex
“Cambrex serves as a critical partner to the world’s most innovative pharma companies to provide reliable and life-saving products to patients globally.”
Henry Minello - Partner at Permira

Cambrex is a leading contract development and manufacturing organisation (CDMO) in the pharmaceutical industry.

The business partners with global pharmaceutical companies to research, develop and manufacture small molecule therapeutics at every stage of the drug lifecycle, with a focus on branded ‘innovator’ drugs.
